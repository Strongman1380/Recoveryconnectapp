From Rock Bottom to Recovery: Michael's Journey

"After 15 years of alcohol addiction, I hit rock bottom when I lost my job and my family threatened to leave. That was my wake-up call. Through therapy, support groups, and the incredible community here at Recovery Connect, I've been sober for 3 years now. My family relationships are healing, and I've found purpose in helping others on their recovery journey."

Michael shares how finding the right support system and developing healthy coping mechanisms transformed his life and helped him rebuild what he had lost.
