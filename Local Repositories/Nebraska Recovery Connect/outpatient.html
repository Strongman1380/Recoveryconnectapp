Find flexible outpatient treatment options that fit your schedule and needs.

What are Outpatient Services?

Outpatient behavioral health services provide treatment for mental health conditions and substance use disorders without requiring you to stay overnight at a facility. These services allow you to receive care while continuing to live at home and maintaining your daily responsibilities.

Types of Outpatient Services

  • Standard Outpatient Programs (SOP): Typically involves one to two therapy sessions per week.
  • Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP): More structured than standard outpatient care, usually involving 9-12 hours of treatment per week.
  • Partial Hospitalization Programs (PHP): The most intensive form of outpatient care, typically involving 20+ hours of treatment per week.
  • Medication Management: Regular appointments with a psychiatrist or other prescriber to monitor medications.
  • Telehealth Services: Remote therapy and treatment services accessed via video conferencing.
