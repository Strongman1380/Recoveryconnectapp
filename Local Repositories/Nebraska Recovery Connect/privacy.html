Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Nebraska Treatment Connect ("we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our service (the "Service").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Service, including:

  • Personal Information: For treatment providers, we collect name, email address, phone number, job title, and organization information.
  • Facility Information: Information about treatment facilities including location, services offered, availability status, and contact details.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our Service, such as search queries and pages visited.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, browser, and how you access our Service.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information directly from you when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Input or update facility information
  • Search for treatment facilities
  • Use various features of our Service

We also collect information automatically as you navigate through the Service using cookies and similar technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Connect individuals with appropriate treatment facilities
  • Allow providers to update facility information and availability
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Enforce our terms, conditions, and policies

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your information:

  • To the public, when you provide facility information intended for public display
  • To contractors, service providers, and other third parties we use to support our business
  • To comply with any court order, law, or legal process
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • If we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others

5. Data Security

We implement measures designed to protect your personal information from accidental loss and unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You can review and update your account information at any time by logging into your account. You may also contact us to request access to, correct, or delete any personal information that you have provided to us.

7. Special Note About Health Information

Our Service is designed to help individuals find treatment facilities. While we do not collect protected health information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA, we take privacy very seriously. We do not collect specific details about an individual's health conditions, treatments sought, or other sensitive personal health information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the Service or by other means.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@nebraskatreatmentconnect.org.
