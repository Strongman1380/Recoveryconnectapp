PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy and confidentiality are our highest priorities

Your Recovery Journey Remains Private & Secure
Effective Date: May 29, 2025
Last Updated: May 29, 2025

Introduction

Recovery Connect ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Recovery Connect application and related services.

Anonymous Access
Participate anonymously in our community while maintaining your privacy
Data Protection
Industry-standard encryption and security measures protect your information
No Data Sales
We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties
Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

We collect information you voluntarily provide when creating an account, participating in community features, or contacting support. This may include your name, email address, recovery milestones, location (if shared), and communication preferences.

Usage Information:

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our application, including features accessed, time spent in different sections, and general usage patterns to improve our services.

Device Information:

We may collect device-specific information such as device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information to ensure optimal application performance.

Community Interaction Data:

Messages, posts, and interactions within our community chat and support features are collected to maintain community safety and provide requested services.

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision:

Your information helps us provide personalized recovery resources, connect you with appropriate support materials, and facilitate meaningful community connections.

Safety and Security:

We use your information to maintain a safe recovery environment, prevent misuse of our platform, and protect against harmful or inappropriate content.

Communication:

We may use your contact information to send important updates about our services, recovery resources, or respond to your inquiries and support requests.

Improvement and Development:

Aggregated, non-identifying usage data helps us understand user needs and improve our recovery support features and resources.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

No Sale of Personal Data:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

Service Providers:

We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our application, conducting business, or serving users, provided they agree to keep information confidential.

Legal Requirements:

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users or the public, including situations involving imminent harm or danger.

Recovery Professional Referrals:

With your explicit consent, we may share relevant information with licensed recovery professionals or treatment providers you choose to connect with through our platform.

Data Security and Protection

Security Measures:

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Recovery-Specific Protections:

Given the sensitive nature of recovery information, we employ additional safeguards including limited staff access, confidentiality agreements, and specialized data handling procedures.

Data Retention:

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Correction:

You have the right to access, update, or correct your personal information through your account settings or by contacting our support team.

Data Portability:

You may request a copy of your personal data in a commonly used format.

Deletion Rights:

You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal requirements and legitimate business interests.

Communication Preferences:

You can opt out of non-essential communications while maintaining access to critical safety and service-related messages.

Special Considerations for Recovery Services

Confidentiality Commitment:

We understand the sensitive nature of recovery journeys and commit to maintaining the highest standards of confidentiality consistent with applicable laws and ethical guidelines.

Crisis Intervention:

In situations involving imminent danger or harm, we may need to share information with appropriate authorities or emergency services to ensure safety.

Professional Referrals:

Our platform may connect you with licensed professionals, and we encourage users to understand the separate privacy policies and confidentiality practices of these providers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through in-app notifications or email communications.
