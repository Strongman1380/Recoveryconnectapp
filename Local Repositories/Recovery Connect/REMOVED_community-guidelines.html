COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Creating a safe, supportive, and empowering recovery community

Effective Date: May 29, 2025
Last Updated: May 29, 2025

Our Community Mission

Recovery Connect exists to provide a safe, supportive, and empowering space for individuals on their recovery journey. Our community guidelines ensure that all users can participate in meaningful connections while maintaining respect, safety, and mutual support.

Core Community Values

Respect and Dignity:

Every community member deserves to be treated with respect regardless of their recovery stage, background, or personal circumstances. We celebrate diversity and honor each person's unique journey.

Confidentiality and Privacy:

What is shared in our community stays in our community. Respect others' privacy and do not share personal information or stories outside the platform without explicit permission.

Supportive Communication:

Focus on encouragement, understanding, and constructive support. Share your experiences to help others while respecting that everyone's recovery path is different.

Safety First:

Our community prioritizes physical and emotional safety. If you encounter content that concerns you or indicates someone may be in danger, please report it immediately.

Acceptable Use Standards

Encouraged Behaviors:

Share your recovery victories, challenges, and insights to support others. Ask questions, offer encouragement, and participate in discussions that promote healing and growth. Use respectful language and maintain appropriate boundaries in all interactions.

Resource Sharing:

Share helpful recovery resources, tools, and information that may benefit others. Ensure shared content is accurate, appropriate, and adds value to our community discussions.

Constructive Feedback:

When offering advice or feedback, do so with kindness and respect. Remember that unsolicited advice may not always be welcome, and focus on sharing your own experience rather than telling others what to do.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

Harmful Content:

Do not post content that promotes self-harm, substance use, illegal activities, or anything that could trigger relapse or harmful behaviors in others. This includes detailed descriptions of substance use or self-destructive behaviors.

Harassment and Abuse:

Harassment, bullying, discriminatory language, hate speech, or personal attacks of any kind are strictly prohibited. This includes content targeting individuals based on race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or recovery status.

Privacy Violations:

Do not share other users' personal information, screenshots of private conversations, or identify other community members outside the platform without their explicit consent.

Commercial Activity:

Our community is not a marketplace. Solicitation, advertising, spam, or commercial promotion is prohibited unless specifically authorized by Recovery Connect administration.

Misinformation:

Do not share false or misleading information about recovery, treatment, medications, or health-related topics. When sharing recovery resources, ensure they come from credible sources.

Crisis Support and Safety Protocols

Immediate Danger:

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, contact emergency services (911) immediately. Our platform is not equipped to handle crisis situations requiring immediate intervention.

Concerning Content:

Report any content that suggests someone may be considering self-harm, relapse, or dangerous behaviors. Our moderation team is trained to respond appropriately and connect users with professional help when needed.

Professional Support:

While our community provides peer support, it is not a substitute for professional treatment. We encourage users to maintain relationships with qualified healthcare providers and treatment professionals.

Community Moderation

Content Review:

Our moderation team reviews reported content and may remove posts or comments that violate these guidelines. Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent account suspension.

User Reporting:

Community members can report concerning content or behavior through our reporting system. All reports are reviewed promptly and handled with appropriate confidentiality.

Appeals Process:

Users who believe their content was removed in error or who wish to appeal a moderation decision may contact our support team for review.

Consequences for Violations

Warning System:

First-time minor violations typically result in a warning and educational guidance about our community standards.

Temporary Suspension:

Serious violations or repeated minor violations may result in temporary account suspension with opportunity for reinstatement after demonstrating understanding of guidelines.

Permanent Removal:

Severe violations involving harassment, safety threats, or repeated disregard for community standards may result in permanent account termination.

Reporting and Support

To report concerning content or behavior:

  • Use the in-app reporting feature
  • Email: support@recoveryconnect.app
  • Contact our crisis support line: 1-800-RECOVERY (732-6837)

Community Guidelines Updates

These guidelines may be updated to reflect community needs and platform developments. Users will be notified of significant changes and are encouraged to review updates regularly.
